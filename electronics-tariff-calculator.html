Electronics Tariffs Explained ()

Electronics face some of the most significant tariff impacts. China manufactures the vast majority of consumer electronics, and those products now face 54%+ combined tariff rates.

Gaming Consoles

  • PlayStation 5 Pro ($699, Japan): ~$130 in tariffs at 24% Japan rate
  • Nintendo Switch 2 ($449, China): ~$110 in tariffs at 54% China rate
  • Xbox Series X ($499, China): ~$120 in tariffs at 54% China rate

TVs & Monitors

Samsung (South Korea, 25%), LG (South Korea, 25%), Sony (Japan, 24%), and Chinese-brand TVs (54%+) all face significant tariffs. Budget TV brands assembled in China are hit hardest.

Laptop Tariffs by Brand

  • Apple MacBook (China, 54%): MacBook Air M4 ($1,299) — ~$250 tariff cost. MacBook Pro ($1,999-$3,499) — $380-$670 tariff cost.
  • Dell XPS (China, 54%): XPS 13 ($1,299) — ~$250 tariff cost. XPS 15 ($1,499) — ~$288 tariff cost.
  • HP Spectre (China, 54%): HP Spectre x360 ($1,399) — ~$268 tariff cost.
  • Lenovo ThinkPad (China, 54%): ThinkPad X1 Carbon ($1,449) — ~$278 tariff cost.
  • ASUS ZenBook (Taiwan, 32%): ZenBook 14 ($999) — ~$175 tariff cost (lower than China-made).

📋 Tips for Laptop Buyers

  • Consider refurbished laptops from Apple, Dell, or Best Buy to avoid tariff markup
  • Taiwan-made laptops (ASUS, Acer) have lower tariff rates than China-made
  • Wait for sales events (back-to-school, Black Friday) when retailers absorb tariff costs
  • Check if the laptop model is available from India assembly (some newer models)

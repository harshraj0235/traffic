Understanding iPhone Tariffs in

Apple's iPhone is one of the most high-profile products affected by US-China tariffs. Despite being designed in California, the vast majority of iPhones are assembled at Foxconn and Pegatron factories in China, making them subject to the full China tariff rate.

iPhone Models and Tariff Impact

Here's how tariffs affect each current iPhone model:

  • iPhone 16 Pro Max ($1,199): Approximately $230-$280 in tariff costs
  • iPhone 16 Pro ($999): Approximately $190-$230 in tariff costs
  • iPhone 16 ($799): Approximately $150-$185 in tariff costs
  • iPhone SE ($429): Approximately $80-$100 in tariff costs
  • AirPods Pro ($249): Approximately $47-$58 in tariff costs
  • Apple Watch Ultra ($799): Approximately $150-$185 in tariff costs

Is Apple Moving iPhone Production Out of China?

Apple has been diversifying its supply chain. India (via Tata Electronics and Foxconn) now produces a growing percentage of iPhones, primarily the standard models. However, Pro models and most other Apple products remain China-assembled. Even India-assembled iPhones face a 26% tariff rate, though this is significantly lower than China's 54%+ rate.

📋 Key iPhone Tariff Facts

  • Most iPhones are assembled in China (Foxconn, Zhengzhou)
  • Effective tariff rate: 54%+ (30% base + 24% Section 301)
  • India-assembled iPhones face 26% tariff (lower but still significant)
  • Apple has absorbed some tariff costs to keep retail prices stable
  • Accessories (cases, chargers) face the same China tariff rates
  • Refurbished iPhones purchased domestically avoid additional tariffs

iPhone Tariff FAQ

Common questions about tariffs on iPhones and Apple products.

How much tariff is on an iPhone?+

iPhones assembled in China face an effective tariff rate of approximately 54% (30% base China tariff + 24% Section 301 electronics duty). On a $999 iPhone 16 Pro, that's roughly $190-$230 in tariff costs built into the retail price.

Why are iPhones still the same price if tariffs are so high?+

Apple has partially absorbed tariff costs to maintain competitive pricing, which reduces their profit margins. Additionally, some analysts believe Apple had built up pre-tariff inventory. However, prices have gradually crept up — the iPhone 16 Pro starts at $999 vs. older models that started at $899-$949.

Are iPhones made in India tariff-free?+

No. India-assembled iPhones face a 26% reciprocal tariff rate — lower than China's 54%+, but still significant. Apple is expanding India production partly to reduce tariff exposure, but the savings aren't passed directly to consumers yet.

Should I buy a refurbished iPhone to avoid tariffs?+

Buying a refurbished iPhone from a domestic seller (Apple Refurbished, Best Buy, etc.) means the tariff was already paid on the original import. You benefit from a lower price without paying additional tariff costs. This is one of the most effective tariff-saving strategies.

Do AirPods and Apple Watch also have tariffs?+

Yes. AirPods are assembled in China and face the same 54%+ tariff rate. Apple Watch is also primarily China-assembled. All Apple accessories (cases, chargers, cables) manufactured in China face full tariff rates.
